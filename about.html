PF-1 ("Profane fest") is an online-based fully-fledged synthesizer.

It's composed of one oscillator, one filter and two envelopes. The amplitude of the oscillator and the cutoff frequency of the filter are controlled by the envelopes.

CONTROLS

Play using your mouse or the top two letter rows of your keyboard.

The octave can be lowered with the Z key and raised with the X key.

Params controlled with a slider can be restored to their original values by double clicking.
